Prepare Employee Dataset

Follow these 3 simple steps to make your file ready for processing.

  • 📄 CSV UTF-8, comma delimiter
  • 🔢 18 required columns + 1 optional
  • ⏱️ Real-time validation
  1. 1

    Download Template

    Use UTF-8 encoding & comma delimiter.
    Example: 5 rows 
    EmployeeName,Age,Department,JobRole,JobLevel,MonthlyIncome,OverTime,WorkLifeBalance,YearsAtCompany,YearsWithCurrManager
"John D",32,"Sales","Sales Executive",2,8400,Yes,Good,4,2
"Anna S",29,"R&D","Research Scientist",1,6100,No,Better,2,1
"Michael B",41,"HR","Human Resources",3,12500,No,Best,8,5
"Sarah T",35,"R&D","Laboratory Technician",2,7200,Yes,Satisfied,5,3
"David R",26,"Sales","Sales Representative",1,5200,No,Good,1,1
    Download Template CSV View Full Structure →
  2. 2

    Fill the Columns

    18 required columns + 1 optional (EmployeeName). Headers must be case-sensitive.

    • Demographics (Age, MaritalStatus)
    • Role & Level (JobRole, JobLevel)
    • Compensation (MonthlyIncome, DailyRate)
    • Work-Life Balance (OverTime, WorkLifeBalance)
    • Tenure (YearsAtCompany, YearsWithCurrManager)

    Supported Values (Categories)

    Department
    Human Resources; Research & Development; Sales
    JobLevel
    Level 1–5
    OverTime
    Yes; No
    WorkLifeBalance
    Bad; Good; Better; Best
    JobRole
    HR; Laboratory Technician; Manager; Manufacturing Director; Research Director; Research Scientist; Sales Executive; Sales Representative; Healthcare Representative
  3. 3

    Upload & Validate

    The system checks headers, data types, and unknown categories.

    Open Upload Form Real-time validation

    Final file: .csv UTF-8, comma, max. 5MB / 50k rows.
