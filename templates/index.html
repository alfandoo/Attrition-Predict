Tentang AttritionPredict

AttritionPredict adalah aplikasi berbasis Machine Learning yang membantu departemen HR memprediksi kemungkinan karyawan meninggalkan perusahaan secara cepat dan akurat.

  • ✔ Analisis cepat & akurat
  • 📊 Prediksi berbasis data nyata
  • 💡 Rekomendasi langkah preventif

Siapkan Dataset Karyawan

Ikuti 3 langkah singkat agar file langsung siap diproses.

  • 📄 CSV UTF-8, delimiter koma
  • 🔢 18 kolom wajib + 1 opsional
  • ⏱️ Validasi real-time
  1. 1

    Unduh Template

    Gunakan encoding UTF-8 & delimiter koma.
    Contoh 5 baris 
    EmployeeName,Age,Department,JobRole,JobLevel,MonthlyIncome,OverTime,WorkLifeBalance,YearsAtCompany,YearsWithCurrManager
"Rizky A",32,"Sales","Sales Executive",2,8400,Yes,Good,4,2
"Vania D",29,"R&D","Research Scientist",1,6100,No,Better,2,1
"Adit K",41,"HR","Human Resources",3,12500,No,Best,8,5
"Intan P",35,"R&D","Laboratory Technician",2,7200,Yes,Satisfied,5,3
"Rudi S",26,"Sales","Sales Representative",1,5200,No,Good,1,1
    Download Template CSV Lihat Struktur Lengkap →
  2. 2

    Lengkapi Kolom

    18 kolom wajib + 1 opsional (EmployeeName). Header harus case-sensitive.

    • Demografi (Age, MaritalStatus)
    • Peran & Level (JobRole, JobLevel)
    • Kompensasi (MonthlyIncome, DailyRate)
    • Keseimbangan (OverTime, WorkLifeBalance)
    • Tenure (YearsAtCompany, YearsWithCurrManager)

    Nilai yang Didukung (Kategori)

    Department
    Human Resources; Research & Development; Sales
    JobLevel
    Level 1–5
    OverTime
    Yes; No
    WorkLifeBalance
    Bad; Good; Better; Best
    JobRole
    HR; Laboratory Technician; Manager; Manufacturing Director; Research Director; Research Scientist; Sales Executive; Sales Representative; Healthcare Representative
  3. 3

    Unggah & Validasi

    Sistem memeriksa header, tipe data, dan kategori tidak dikenal.

    Buka Form Upload Validasi real-time

    File akhir: .csv UTF-8, koma, maks. 5MB / 50k baris.

Cara Kerja

Tiga langkah sederhana dari input data hingga keputusan.

1

Kumpulkan Data

Masukkan usia, jabatan, pendapatan, dll. atau gunakan Template CSV.

Format: CSV UTF-8, delimiter koma.
2

Analisis AI

Model memproses data & menghasilkan prediksi risiko attrition + confidence.

Akurasi & F1 divalidasi cross-val.
3

Ambil Keputusan

Gunakan rekomendasi retensi dan unduh laporan/hasil untuk dibagikan.

Tersedia ringkasan & export.
